Our Mission

Better Outcomes

Our team, DiscOva, aims to revolutionize ovarian cancer screening, and Women’s Health in general, by harnessing the untapped diagnostic screening potential of menstrual effluent – the blood and tissue shed during menstruation. By taking advantage of this novel and promising biological sample, DiscOva will save millions of lives by allowing clinicians to make more informed decisions about patients at high risk of ovarian cancer and many other conditions.


More Comfort

At DiscOva, we are laser focused on taking a customer-centric approach that minimizes the burden of labor on the patient, and prioritizes design requirements identified through extensive customer interviews. The team has spent months listening to clinicians, lab testing experts, and patients alike to determine the most important design requirements and validating design ideas.
